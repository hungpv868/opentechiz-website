We walk through your workflows and surface which bottlenecks cost the most — in time, headcount, or errors. You get a ranked automation roadmap with specific ROI projections, not generic advice.

We define exactly what the AI will do, what data it will use, and where it connects to your stack. Use cases, data flows, and integration points are scoped and agreed before any code is written.
